# 07 – Probability (Born Rule)

Projection valuation \(\mu\) satisfying normalization, additivity on orthogonal families,
noncontextuality, and unitary invariance ⇒ \(\mu(P)=\mathrm{Tr}(\rho P)\).

Pure states ⇒ \(|\langle i|\psi\rangle|^2\).
