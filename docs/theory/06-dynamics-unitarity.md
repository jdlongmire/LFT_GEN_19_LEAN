# 06 – Dynamics and Unitarity

Assume coherence-invariant, strongly continuous evolution \(T_t\).
- Wigner near-identity ⇒ unitary (not antiunitary)
- Stone ⇒ \(U(t)=e^{-itH}\) with self-adjoint \(H\)
